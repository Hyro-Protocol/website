# Vision & Mission
Democratize asset management by creating a trustless, transparent, and efficient capital allocation ecosystem that enables direct relationships between liquidity providers and skilled managers.
* Use smart contracts to eliminate intermediaries in asset management.
* Enable transparent, verifiable performance tracking and capital allocation.
* Lower entry barriers for new managers while safeguarding LP interests.
* Build a meritocratic system driven by provable performance.
* Facilitate seamless capital discovery and deployment via decentralized infrastructure.
